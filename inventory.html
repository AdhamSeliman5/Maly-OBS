Your Product Catalog & Stock at a Glance

The Inventory module manages your product catalog and stock levels. Add and edit products with variants, record stock purchases, run stock adjustments (stocktake, damaged, gift), process quarantine items (valid vs damaged), and view analytics. Restock alerts show items with negative available stock. Bundles are virtual—availability is computed from component stock.

  • Products table: Physical, Reserved, Available, Cost, with search and category filter
  • Purchase stock, run adjustments (Stocktake, Damaged, Gift), process quarantine
  • Analytics tab: sold units, revenue, profit, damage rate; Top Profit & Most Damaged lists

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

Product Catalog

Add and edit products with variants (name, price). Buy, Edit, Delete per product. Bundles are read-only—availability computed from components.

Stock Purchases

Record purchases with supplier, quantity, cost per variant. System updates WAC, records expense if Paid > 0, creates Receivable/Payable when needed.

Stock Adjustment

Select product, variant, quantity (+/−), type (Stocktake, Damaged, Gift, Correction). Damaged uses negative qty. Reason optional.

Quarantine & Analytics

Process quarantine: split Valid (return to stock) vs Damaged. Analytics tab: KPIs, date range, Top Profit, Most Damaged. Stock Movements history.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

Add a New Product

Click New Product. Enter Product Name. Add variants (name + price), click + for each. Click Save. At least one variant required; duplicate names show error.

2

Purchase Stock

Click the Buy icon on a product row. Enter Supplier (required), then for each variant: Quantity, Cost, optionally Paid. Click Confirm. Treasury must be sufficient if Paid > 0.

3

Stock Adjustment

Click Adjustment in the header. Select Product and Variant. Enter quantity change (+ or −). Choose type: Stocktake, Damaged, Gift, Correction. For Damaged, use negative quantity.

4

Process Quarantine

Go to Quarantine tab. Click Process on an item. Split into Valid (return to stock) and Damaged. Valid + Damaged must equal total. Optionally add refurbishment cost. Confirm.
