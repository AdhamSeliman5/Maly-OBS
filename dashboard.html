See Your Business at a Glance

The Dashboard is the main analytics screen in Maly - OBS. It shows your business performance at a glance using key numbers (KPIs), charts, and lists. Choose a date range to see sales, deliveries, shipping, returns, top products, top governorates, customer insights, and campaign performance. Sync orders from Google Sheets to keep your data up to date.

  • Live KPI cards: Revenue, Delivered, To Prepare, In Shipping, Returned, Active Clients
  • Interactive charts: Sales Trend, Status pie, Shipping Performance, Top Sellers
  • Sync orders from Google Sheets with one click—data stays current across your workflow

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

KPI Cards

Total Revenue, Delivered, To Prepare, In Shipping, Returned, and Active Clients at a glance. Click To Prepare or In Shipping to jump to Preparation or Shipping.

Date Range Filter

Shows the current date range. Click to open the date picker, choose Start and End dates, then Apply. All dashboard data refreshes for the new range.

Sync Orders

Fetch the latest orders from Google Sheets with one click. A full-screen overlay shows sync progress. When done, the dashboard refreshes automatically.

Charts & Lists

Sales Trend, Top Sellers, Status pie chart, Order Types, Shipping Performance, Avg Delivery Days. Expand More Analytics for governorates, VIPs, and campaigns.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

View the Dashboard

Open the Dashboard. The system loads KPIs first, then charts. Default date range is start of year to today unless you previously set another range.

2

Change the Date Range

Click the Date Range button. A date-picker dialog opens. Choose Start Date and End Date, then click Apply. The system reloads all dashboard data for the new range.

3

Sync Orders from Google Sheets

Click Sync Orders. A full-screen overlay appears with "Syncing with Google Sheets...". On success, you'll see "Sync Complete" and the dashboard refreshes. On failure, an error message is shown.

4

Navigate to Preparation or Shipping

Click the To Prepare card to open the Preparation module, or the In Shipping card to open the Shipping module—seamless navigation between workflows.
