User Guide

Preparation Hub

Maly - OBS (Online Business System) · Version 1.0

Module Purpose

The Preparation module manages orders that are ready to be picked and packed but have not yet been handed over to a shipping company. You can mark orders as prepared, see which ones have enough stock, defer or cancel orders, modify bundles, and export prepared orders to Excel or ship them to a courier. Deferred orders appear in a separate tab and "wake up" a configurable number of days before their due date.

Step-by-Step Workflow

1. View and Filter Orders

  • Open the Preparation module.
  • Use Search to find orders by code, name, phone, or products.
  • Click Filter by Governorate to limit by governorate(s) → select → Confirm.
  • Click Filter by Type to show All, Ready, Exchange, Missing, or Shortage only.
  • Use pagination if there are many orders.

2. Mark an Order as Prepared

Single order

  • Ensure the order shows Ready (stock indicator green or sufficient).
  • Click Prepare on the order card.
  • The system checks stock. If OK, the order is marked prepared and the list refreshes.
  • If stock is insufficient, you see: "Insufficient stock for: [product name]".

Bulk (auto-prepare)

  • Click Auto-Prepare Options in the right panel.
  • Choose Current Page (this page only) or All Pending.
  • The system prepares all eligible orders.
  • A snackbar shows: "Auto-prepared orders: [count]".

3. Unmark a Prepared Order

  • Click Undo on a prepared order card.
  • The order returns to pending (not prepared).

Unmark all

  • Click Unmark All in the right panel.
  • Confirm in the dialog.
  • All prepared orders are unmarked.

4. Defer an Order

  • Open the ⋮ menu on the order.
  • Choose Defer Order.
  • Select a due date in the date picker.
  • Click Defer.
  • The order moves to the Deferred tab and its stock reservation is released.

5. Cancel an Order

  • Open the ⋮ menu on the order.
  • Choose Cancel Order.
  • Confirm in the dialog.
  • The order is cancelled, its status updates in the system and Google Sheets, and its stock reservation is released.

6. Modify a Bundle (Unbox)

  • Open the ⋮ menu on an order that has bundles.
  • Choose Modify Bundle.
  • Adjust quantities with +/- or type in the field.
  • Click the delete icon to remove a component (sets quantity to 0).
  • At least one component must remain — otherwise you get: "Keep at least one component".
  • Click Confirm Unbox.
  • The bundle is replaced by the chosen components and quantities.

7. Export / Ship Prepared Orders

  • Ensure at least one order is marked prepared.
  • Click Export Options (X) where X is the count of prepared orders.
  • Select Courier (shipping company).
  • Pick Ship Date.
  • Excel Only — generate Excel file only (no status change).
  • Ship & Export — update orders to Shipped, assign courier and ship date, deduct stock, export Excel, and sync to Google Sheets.
  • If using Ship & Export, courier is required; otherwise you see an error.

8. Sync Orders from Google Sheets

  • Click the Cloud Sync icon in the header.
  • A loading overlay appears.
  • When done, a snackbar shows success or error and the list refreshes.

9. Adjust Deferred Orders Settings

  • Click Settings in the header.
  • Set Days before due date (e.g. 5).
  • Click Save.
  • Deferred orders appear in the main Orders tab when their due date is within that many days.

Business Rules & Validations

Required Conditions to Prepare

  • Order must be Ready (stock status = available).
  • Order must have no unknown items (all items mapped to variants).
  • Current stock (minus reserved) must be sufficient for the order quantity.

Prepare Validation

ConditionResult
Insufficient stockError: "Insufficient stock for: [product name]"
Order has unknown itemsPrepare button disabled
Order not Ready (shortage)Prepare button disabled

Defer / Cancel

  • Defer and Cancel release reserved stock immediately.
  • Cancelled orders are excluded from the preparation list.
  • Cancellation syncs status to Google Sheets (e.g. ملغى).

Bundle Explosion (Unbox)

  • At least one component must have quantity > 0; otherwise: "Keep at least one component".
  • After unbox, the order is marked as manually modified so sync does not overwrite it.

Tips

  • Use the Star to mark urgent orders; they appear first in the list.
  • Stock indicator (e.g. 3/5) shows how many item types are in stock versus total.
  • For shortage, use Show Shortage to see exactly what is missing.
  • Deferred tab shows orders scheduled for later with countdown (green = OK, yellow = soon, red = due/overdue).
  • Picking List aggregates quantities across all prepared orders for efficient picking.
  • Export Excel Only when you only need a file without changing order status.
  • Use Ship & Export when you hand over to a courier and want status updated in the system and Sheets.
