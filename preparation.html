Pick, Pack, and Ship with Confidence

The Preparation module manages orders ready to be picked and packed but not yet handed over to a courier. Mark orders as prepared, see which have enough stock, defer or cancel orders, modify bundles (unbox), and export to Excel or ship to a courier. Deferred orders appear in a separate tab and wake up a configurable number of days before their due date.

  • Stat badges: Total, Ready, Prepared, Shortage, Exchange/Missing. Filter by governorate or type.
  • Prepare (single or Auto-Prepare), Unmark, Defer, Cancel. Modify Bundle to unbox components.
  • Export: Excel Only or Ship & Export. Sync from Google Sheets. Picking List for grouped quantities.

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

Order Picking & Prepare

Mark orders as prepared when stock is Ready. Stock indicator (e.g. 3/5) shows items in stock vs total. Auto-Prepare: Current Page or All Pending.

Defer & Cancel

Defer: pick due date—order moves to Deferred tab, stock released. Cancel: confirm—order cancelled, status synced to Sheets, stock released.

Bundle Unbox

Modify Bundle from ⋮ menu. Adjust component quantities with +/- or type. Remove items (delete sets qty to 0). At least one component must remain. Confirm Unbox.

Export & Ship

Export Options: Excel Only (no status change) or Ship & Export (update to Shipped, assign courier, deduct stock, sync Sheets). Courier required for Ship.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

View and Filter Orders

Open Preparation. Use Search (code, name, products). Filter by Governorate or Type (All, Ready, Exchange, Missing, Shortage). Paginate through the list.

2

Mark Orders as Prepared

Ensure order shows Ready (stock sufficient). Click Prepare on the card. If stock insufficient: "Insufficient stock for: [product]". Or use Auto-Prepare for Current Page or All Pending.

3

Defer or Cancel

From ⋮ menu: Defer—select due date, order moves to Deferred tab. Cancel—confirm, order cancelled and synced. Both release reserved stock.

4

Export or Ship Prepared Orders

Click Export Options. Choose Courier and Ship Date. Excel Only: file only. Ship & Export: status Shipped, stock deducted, Excel generated, Sheets synced. Courier required for Ship.
