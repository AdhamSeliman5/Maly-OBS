Your Full Financial Position at a Glance

The Debts module shows your complete financial position: liquid assets (receivables, pipeline, shipping, staff loans), fixed assets, and liabilities (payables, staff due, shipping debt). Add manual debts, settle full or partial, manage fixed assets, and use the forecast simulator for delivery rate and pipeline.

  • Liquid assets: Receivables, Pipeline, Shipping, Staff Loans; Liabilities: Payables, Staff Due
  • Add manual Receivable/Payable; Settle full or partial; View settlement history
  • Fixed assets: Add, Update, Sell; Forecast simulator for delivery rate and pipeline

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

Financial Position

View liquid assets (receivables, pipeline, shipping, staff loans) and liabilities (payables, staff due, shipping debt) in one dashboard.

Add & Settle Debts

Add manual Receivable or Payable. Settle in full or partial amounts. View complete settlement history per debt.

Fixed Assets

Add, update, or sell fixed assets. Track asset value and depreciation. Integrates with your overall financial position.

Forecast Simulator

Adjust delivery rate and pipeline assumptions. Simulate future cash flow based on expected receivables and payables.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

Add a Debt

Click Add Debt. Choose Receivable or Payable. Enter party name, amount, optional due date and notes. Confirm. The debt appears in the appropriate asset or liability section.

2

Settle Full or Partial

Click Settle on a debt. Enter amount (full or partial). Partial settlement reduces the remaining balance. View history of all settlements for each debt.

3

Manage Fixed Assets

Add a fixed asset with name, value, and optional details. Update when needed. Sell to record disposal and update financial position accordingly.

4

Adjust Forecast

Use the forecast simulator to adjust delivery rate and pipeline assumptions. See projected cash flow and financial position based on your inputs.
