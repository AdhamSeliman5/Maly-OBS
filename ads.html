Campaigns, Spend & ROI at Your Fingertips

The Ads module manages advertising campaigns, daily spend tracking, and marketing analytics. Create campaigns per platform (Facebook, Google, TikTok, Snapchat), set budgets, record daily spend, and record ad charges (platform top-ups) as expenses. Analytics shows ROAS, CPA, CPI, campaign delivery rate, and exportable PDF reports.

  • Campaigns Manager: Name, Platform, Budget, Total Spend, Active/Paused with Edit & Delete
  • Daily Spend Entry with Copy Previous Day; New Charge for platform top-ups (recorded as expense)
  • Analytics: KPIs, Platform Breakdown, charts (Bar/Line), Campaign Performance, PDF export

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

Campaign Management

Create campaigns for Facebook, Google, TikTok, Snapchat. Set Name, Platform, Budget. Edit, Delete, or toggle Active/Paused. Budget exceeded warning when spend > budget.

Daily Spend & Charges

Record daily spend per active campaign. Copy Previous Day to speed data entry. New Charge records platform top-ups as protected Ads expenses.

Analytics & ROI

Total Spend, Revenue, ROAS, CPA, CPI, Campaign Delivery Rate. Filter by date range, platform, campaign. Platform Breakdown table with sortable columns.

Charts & PDF Export

Bar and Line chart views for Spend, Revenue, ROAS, CPA over time. Export Analytics to PDF with current filters—stats, charts, and platform breakdown included.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

Add a Campaign

Open Campaigns Manager. Click New Campaign. Enter Name, select Platform (Facebook, Google, TikTok, Snapchat), set Budget, toggle Active. Save. Edit or Delete via row actions.

2

Record New Charge (Platform Top-Up)

Click New Charge. Select Platform, enter Amount, adjust Date if needed. Save. The charge is recorded as an Ads expense and cannot be edited. Treasury must be sufficient.

3

Enter Daily Spend

Open Daily Spend Entry. Select date. Enter spend per active campaign (0 if none). Use Copy Previous Day to copy yesterday's values. Save. Negative values are treated as 0.

4

View Analytics & Export PDF

Open Analytics & ROI. Set Date Range, Platform, and Campaign filters. View KPIs, Platform Breakdown, charts (toggle Bar/Line), and Campaign Performance table. Click Export PDF to generate a report.
