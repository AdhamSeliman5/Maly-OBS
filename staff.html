Staff & HR / Employees & Payroll

Maly - OBS (Online Business System) · Version 1.0

Module Purpose

The Staff module manages employees, payroll, and salary-related actions. You add employees with roles (Manager, Moderator, Staff, Accountant, Marketer), set salary methods (Fixed, Commission-based, Per Order, Per Item, or Percentage of ad spend), and calculate payroll from accrued salary, commissions, bonuses, deductions, and loans. You can process payments (with optional Google Sheet update), add bonuses, deductions, or loans, and view transaction history. Deleting an employee with an outstanding balance lets you settle first or move the balance to the Debts module.

Step-by-Step Workflow

1. Add an Employee

  • Click Add Employee.
  • Enter Name, Role, Salary Method, Base, Commission (if applicable).
  • Set Hire Date and Last Paid (Hire Date must be ≤ Last Paid Date).
  • For Moderator: select Manager. For Marketer: enter Ad Spend if salary method is Percentage.
  • For Moderator/Manager with commission: choose Commission Scope (Delivered Only or All Orders).
  • Click Save. Success: green snackbar; table refreshes.

2. Edit an Employee

  • In the Team table, click Edit (✎) on the row.
  • Update any fields (Name, Role, Salary Method, Base, Commission, Manager, Ad Spend, Commission Scope).
  • Hire Date and Last Paid cannot be changed when editing.
  • Click Save. Success: green snackbar; table refreshes.

3. Delete an Employee

  • In the Team table, click Delete (🗑).
  • If no outstanding balance: Confirm in the dialog. Employee is removed.
  • If outstanding balance: Choose Settle First (opens Payroll tab) or Move to Debts (moves balance to Debts module and deletes employee).
  • Success: green snackbar (or message about balance moved to Debts).

4. Calculate Payroll

  • Open Payroll & Actions tab.
  • Select Employee from the dropdown.
  • Period From shows automatically (last paid date or 1st of month). Adjust Period To if needed.
  • For Marketer: enter Ad Spend if not already set.
  • Click Calculate Payroll. The right panel shows earnings, deductions, and Net Due.

5. Add Bonus

  • In Payroll tab, select an employee and click Calculate Payroll (optional).
  • Click Bonus. Enter Amount and optionally Notes.
  • Click Add. Success: green snackbar. Recalculate payroll to see the bonus in the breakdown.

6. Add Deduction

  • In Payroll tab, select an employee and click Calculate Payroll (optional).
  • Click Deduction. Enter Amount and optionally Notes.
  • Click Add. Success: green snackbar. Recalculate to see the deduction in the breakdown.

7. Add Loan/Advance

  • In Payroll tab, select an employee and click Calculate Payroll (optional).
  • Click Loan/Advance. Enter Amount and optionally Notes.
  • Click Confirm. The amount is paid from treasury immediately and deducted from future salary.
  • Success: green snackbar; treasury must be sufficient. Recalculate to see the loan in deductions.

8. Process Payment

  • Calculate payroll for the employee. Review Net Due in the result card.
  • Enter Pay Amount (default: full net due; can pay partial — remainder is carried over).
  • For Moderator/Manager with commission orders: check Update Google Sheet to mark orders as paid (if configured).
  • Click Process Payment. Success: green snackbar; payslip PDF opens (saved to Desktop/Payslib/employee_name/).
  • If treasury is insufficient: error snackbar.

9. View History

  • Open History & Logs tab.
  • Filter by Role and Employee. Click Refresh if needed.
  • Table shows Date, Name, Role, Type (e.g., Salary Payment, Loan), Amount, Notes.

Tips

  • Due Today in the Team table shows what is owed up to today (accrued salary, commission, bonus minus deduction, plus carried over).
  • Commission Scope affects whether commission is calculated on delivered orders only or all orders.
  • Moderator commission is based on orders where moderator_name matches; Manager commission is from moderators' orders (those under the manager).
  • Marketer with Percentage method: commission = Ad Spend × (Commission % / 100).
  • Carried Over = positive remainder when you pay less than net due; it appears in next payroll.
  • Loan is paid from treasury immediately; it reduces employee balance (negative) and is shown in deductions.
  • Update Google Sheet syncs moderator settlement status when payment includes commission orders.
  • Payslip PDF is saved to Desktop/Payslib/[Employee Name]/ and opens after a successful payment.
  • Soft delete: Employees with transaction history are marked inactive; others are hard-deleted.
