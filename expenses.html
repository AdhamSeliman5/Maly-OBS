Treasury & Operations Ledger at Your Fingertips

The Expenses module tracks income and expenses, maintains your treasury balance, and provides a date-filtered ledger. Add manual entries, close days (reconcile), and consolidate. Treasury is strictly enforced—no negative balance allowed.

  • Add manual income/expense entries with amount, category, date, and notes
  • KPIs: Total Treasury, Total Income, Total Expenses with date range filter
  • Close Day to reconcile; Consolidate to lock historical periods

Capabilities

What You Can Do

Key features at your fingertips.

Treasury Balance

Strictly enforced cash balance—no negative treasury. All income and expenses flow through this ledger to maintain accurate liquidity tracking.

Manual Entries

Add income or expense entries with amount, category, date, and optional notes. Edit or delete entries as needed. Create custom categories.

Date-Filtered Ledger

Filter by date range to view transactions. KPIs (Total Treasury, Income, Expenses) update based on the selected period.

Close Day & Consolidate

Close Day to reconcile daily operations. Consolidate to lock historical periods and ensure data integrity for reporting.

Workflow

How It Works

Get started in minutes.

1

Add an Entry

Click Add Entry. Choose Income or Expense. Enter amount, select or create a category, set date, add notes if needed. Confirm. Treasury must remain non-negative.

2

Edit or Delete

Use Edit to change amount, category, date, or notes. Delete to remove an entry. Both actions recalculate treasury and ledger immediately.

3

Add Categories & Filter

Add custom categories for income or expense types. Use the date range filter to view ledger and KPIs for specific periods.

4

Close Day & Consolidate

Close Day to reconcile daily totals. When ready, Consolidate to lock historical data and finalize periods for reporting.
