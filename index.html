Modules

Everything Your Business Needs

11 integrated modules covering every aspect of your e-commerce operations.

Real-time Dashboard

Live KPI cards for revenue, delivery rates, returns, and top products. Interactive charts and a custom date-range picker to spot trends before they become problems.

Advanced Inventory

Full stock lifecycle management: variant tracking, real-time stock levels, quarantine processing for damaged goods, weighted average costing, and a complete audit trail of every movement.

Manufacturing & Bundles

Build Bills of Materials (BOMs) to assemble product bundles and kits. Track component availability, auto-calculate bundle costs, clone bundles for quick variations, and monitor profitability per bundle.

Order Preparation

A smart warehouse picking queue with automatic shortage alerts, bundle explosion into component lists, deferred order management, and exportable picking sheets for your warehouse team.

Shipping Management

Manage multiple couriers from one screen. Track every shipment from dispatch to delivery, bulk-process collections, handle returns and partial deliveries, and reconcile each company's balance automatically.

Expenses & Treasury

Log every business expense by category, watch your real-time treasury balance, and close your books daily with variance tracking between system totals and physical cash-on-hand.

Staff & HR Payroll

Full HR suite: role-based access control, fixed or commission-based payroll engine, employee loan tracking, per-moderator performance metrics, bonus and deduction adjustments, and complete payroll history.

Ad Campaign Intelligence

Link ad spend on Facebook, TikTok, Google, and Instagram to real revenue. Calculate ROAS and Cost-Per-Order per campaign and per platform so you know exactly where every pound is working — and where it isn't.

Financial Reports & Scenarios

P&L statements, partner share calculations, period closing (التقفيل), and interactive scenario simulations — "What if 70% of my pipeline delivers?" — so you can make confident financial decisions before they happen.

Google Sheets Sync

Bidirectional sync between your local database and Google Sheets. Your team updates orders in the spreadsheet; Maly pulls them in automatically — no extra software, no manual imports.

Debts & Liabilities

Consolidated view of every obligation: external loans, courier balances pending collection, employee payroll owed, partner equity, and upcoming payment forecasts with an 7-day alert window.

Role-based Access Control

Create multi-user accounts with granular module permissions: managers see everything, staff see only their workspace. Every action is logged in a full audit trail with timestamp and user identity.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Google OAuth & User Data

Maly - OBS connects to your Google Account to facilitate data synchronization. It uses the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets and https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scopes solely to export reports generated by the user to their personal Google Sheets. We do not read, delete, or modify any other files on your Google Drive.

We do not share your Google User Data with any third party, AI model, or analytics service. OAuth access tokens are stored locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

Local Data Storage

All your business data (inventory, orders, staff, and finances) is stored locally on your own machine in a SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your business data on our servers. You own your data completely.
