Google User Data Policy

Maly - OBS connects to your Google Account to facilitate data synchronization. When you grant permissions, the application accesses your Google Sheets solely to export reports (Sales, Inventory, and Financials) generated by you within the app to your personal Google Sheets. We request the ../auth/spreadsheets scope for this purpose.

We do not read, delete, or modify any files on your Google Drive other than those created by Maly - OBS. We do not share your Google User Data with any third-party tools or AI models.

Data is stored locally on your device (SQLite). OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local machine.
