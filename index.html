Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Maly - OBS.

Is my data secure? Where is it stored?

All your business data—inventory, orders, staff, and finances—is stored locally on your own machine in a SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your business data on our servers. You own your data completely, and it never leaves your computer unless you choose to sync with Google Sheets.

Can I use Maly - OBS offline?

Yes. Maly - OBS is a desktop application that runs fully offline. All core modules—inventory, shipping, expenses, staff, and reports—work without an internet connection. You only need internet when syncing orders from Google Sheets or when activating your license.

How does the Google Sheets sync work?

You connect your Google account once, then link a Google Sheet that contains your orders. Maly can push system data (products, governors) to the sheet and pull new orders from it. Your team updates the spreadsheet; Maly syncs bidirectionally. We use only the spreadsheets and drive.file scopes—we do not read or modify any other files on your Google Drive.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer, Vodafone Cash, and other local payment methods in Egypt. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to receive payment details and activate your license. The 1-week free trial requires no payment—just download and start using the system.

Is Maly - OBS available in Arabic?

Yes. Maly - OBS is fully bilingual (English and Arabic). The entire interface, including 500+ strings, supports RTL layout for Arabic. You can switch languages anytime with the language toggle in the navigation bar.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Google OAuth & User Data

Maly - OBS connects to your Google Account to facilitate data synchronization. It uses the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets and https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scopes solely to export reports generated by the user to their personal Google Sheets. We do not read, delete, or modify any other files on your Google Drive.

We do not share your Google User Data with any third party, AI model, or analytics service. OAuth access tokens are stored locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

Local Data Storage

All your business data (inventory, orders, staff, and finances) is stored locally on your own machine in a SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your business data on our servers. You own your data completely.
