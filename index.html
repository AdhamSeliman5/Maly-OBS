Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Maly - OBS ("we", "our", or "us") is a desktop application designed to help businesses manage inventory, manufacturing, and sales. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

2. Data We Collect

Maly - OBS stores all business data (products, customers, sales) locally on your device using an SQLite database. We do not host your data on our servers.

3. Google User Data & Permissions

To provide cloud synchronization features, Maly - OBS requires access to your Google Account. Here is exactly how we use these permissions:

  • View and manage your spreadsheets (../auth/spreadsheets): Used solely to export reports (Sales, Inventory, and Financials) generated by you within the app to your personal Google Sheets.
  • View and manage files created by this app (../auth/drive.file): Used to organize these reports into specific folders within your Drive.

We do not read, delete, or modify any files on your Google Drive other than those created by Maly - OBS. We do not share your Google User Data with any third-party tools or AI models.

4. Security

Your Google OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device using secure encryption methods. They are never transmitted to our servers.
